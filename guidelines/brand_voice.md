# GenLayer — Brand Voice

The condensed copy rules. For the full reference see `brand_guidelines_text.md`.

## Personality

Transparent, trustworthy, built on integrity. Bold and innovative, shaping decentralized AI.
Highly intelligent, reliable, technically superior. Premium — yet accessible to developers
and enterprises.

**Tone pillars:** Factual & Intelligent · Futuristic & Bold · Engaging & Community-Oriented.

## Register

Intelligent, precise, and a little bit grand — GenLayer writes like a civic institution for
AI. Confident declarations about trust, consensus, and adjudication, grounded by concrete,
builder-focused verbs.

- **Person:** mostly *we* and *you*.
- **A recurring device:** triads of self-X verbs — "self-interpreting, self-executing,
  self-enforcing."
- **Named eras:** testnets are named after sci-fi authors — **Asimov** (past), **Bradbury**
  (current). The mascot is **Mochi**. Governance is the **Deepthought DAO**.
- **Judge analogy** is standard: validators, arbiters, jurisdiction.

## Casing

| Context | Rule | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Display / hero titles | Sentence case, loud presence | "The first testnet where AI directly participates in blockchain consensus" |
| Proper nouns | Title Case | Intelligent Contracts · Testnet Bradbury · Optimistic Democracy · Deepthought DAO |
| Labels / CTAs / stat headers | UPPERCASE, `letter-spacing: 0.1em` | `LEADERBOARD` · `VIEW ALL` · `SUBMIT A CONTRIBUTION` |
| Body | Sentence case, normal tracking | — |

Never all-caps for running copy.

## Punctuation

Em-dashes and colons are welcome. Arrows (`→`) end link/CTA text and double as decorative
bullets ("View all →"). Numbers in copy are **digits**, not spelled out (`$5,000`, `20%`,
`Testnet Bradbury 13`).

## Emoji

None. Not in product, not in marketing.

## Lines that are "very GenLayer"

- "A World Where Trust is Autonomous and Universal." *(big-idea register)*
- "Build. Earn. Permanently." *(short, declarative, slightly audacious)*
- "Most hackathons give you a one-time prize. GenLayer gives you a revenue stream." *(to builders)*

## Do not say

leverage · unlock value · revolutionary · game-changing · generic web3 buzzwords.

The brand earns boldness through **specificity** — testnet names, the consensus model,
contract semantics — not marketing adjectives.
