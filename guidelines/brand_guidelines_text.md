Vision
GenLayer© 	Brand Guidelines Document
A World Where Trust is
Autonomous and Universal
Civilization has always advanced by expanding the circle of trust—from
tribes to cities to a global economy. The next leap is imminent: billions of
autonomous AI agents will join that circle, negotiating value and making
decisions faster than any human institution can follow. Without a new
foundation, the gap between human oversight and machine action will
fracture markets, law, and social cohesion. GenLayer exists to weave that new
foundation. We are building a decentralized digital jurisdiction where every
decision, data feed, and contractual clause is validated by a diverse network of
AI arbiters working in transparent consensus.
Intelligent Contracts written in natural language and code become self-
interpreting, self-executing, and self-enforcing. Disputes resolve themselves
in minutes, not months, with no gatekeepers, hidden biases, or geographic
boundaries. Our vision is a world where humans and machines collaborate
under a shared, neutral rule set—one that scales trust as swiftly as information
itself. In this future, creativity and commerce thrive because integrity is
programmable, verifiable, and universally accessible. GenLayer is laying the
cornerstone of that future: an autonomous trust fabric that lets humanity and AI
build together with confidence.

Logo
Misusage
To ensure our logo’s integrity and impact, it’s crucial
to avoid misuse. Here are common mistakes to steer
clear of:
1. Distortion: Don’t alter the logo’s proportions or
stretch it.
2. Color Alteration: Don’t use unauthorized colors or
color combinations.
3. Low Contrast: Ensure colors have sufficient con-
trast for clarity.
4. Sizing: Maintain the minimum size for visibility,
never too small.
5. Obstruction: Don’t cover the logo with other
elements or text.
6. Rotation: Avoid rotating the logo arbitrarily.
By avoiding these pitfalls, we maintain our brand’s
strength and recognition.

GenLayer© 	Brand Guidelines Document	37
Our icon system is crafted for clarity and a
distinctively modern aesthetic. Designed to be
minimal and highly intuitive, GenLayer icons guide
users effortlessly while reinforcing our clean,
sophisticated brand identity.
Detailed & Subtle
On the homepage, you’ll find slightly larger, more
detailed icons. These are designed to be eye-
catching and engaging, providing a subtle depth
that complements our premium visuals without
distracting from key messages.
Compact & Grounded
For all other pages, icons appear in a smaller,
more compact form. To ensure legibility and visual
consistency at these sizes, each icon is presented
within a square frame. This grounding element
provides stability and a unified appearance across
the site, making sure even our smallest visual cues
feel precise and intentional.
This dual approach ensures our icons are always
optimized for their context, delivering clear
information with modern elegance whether they’re
prominent on the homepage or guiding users
through detailed content.
